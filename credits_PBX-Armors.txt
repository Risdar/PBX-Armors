The New Armors in this Addon:

Yellow: Haste + RadSuit, 130% Durability, 50 Damage Reduction

Purple: Quad Damage, 140% Durability, 90 Damage Reduction

Red: Berserk Pack, 100% Durability, 50 Damage Reduction

White: Regeneration for 1 minute stackable up to 200% HP, 200% Durability, 100 Damage Reduction

Orange: Night Vision, 160% Durability, 70 Damage Reduction

Black: Gives you 20 ammo of each existing type, 160% Durability, 70 Damage Reduction

Cyan: Stop/Freeze the time for 12 seconds and buff you speed fire rate for 12 seconds, 40% Durability, 200% Damage Reduction

Demonic: Gives you Berserk, Quad Damage, and the monsters fears you, 300% Durability, 100% Damage Reduction. Only have 10% chance of Spawn in the Tier 4.

The New Armors in this version:


Gold: Acts like a magnet, attracting ammo (including backpacks), health bonus, armor bonus and weapons.

Light Blue: Allows you to breathe underwater indefinitely and move faster while submerged.

Dark Purple: Your ammo is unlimited while wearing the armor.

Pink: Enemies who attack you in melee are repelled and stunned (pain state) for 4 seconds.

Green: It's a lifesaver. When your HP is below 10, it restores your health and armor to maximum (armor protection level is 100%) and grants you an invulnerability sphere.

Dark red/orange: If you touch an enemy, it explodes and reduces durability by 10. This ability ends when the armor breaks. The armor does not degrade from any type of damage other than using the ability.

Gray: This armor always provides random durability and protection values; it will never give you the same amount each time you use it.
